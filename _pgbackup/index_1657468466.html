The Problem...

…is that the building restoration industry has fallen behind most others in its use of modern communication technology. Forensic engineers use decades-old processes and communication tools considered obsolete in other professional service industries. Forensic engineers are producing old-fashioned, barely useful work product at glacial speeds.

Our Vision and Mission…

…is to provide fully customized professional services with ‘zero-day’ turn-around speed. Our team utilizes cutting-edge field equipment and process automation to generate deliverables and advice before leaving the site.

Professional Services

Property Claim Response

We provide useful, accurate advice regarding the origin and cause of the loss, as well as the extent of damage and conceptual repairs before our feet leave the site. We call this zero-day turn-around, and it is our obsession. We have extensive experience serving property insurers on complex and large losses, and we have witnessed the damage wrought by the full range of natural perils.

Repair and Restoration Design

We seek opportunities to shepherd damaged and deteriorated buildings through the entire process of investigation, design, and repair. Our forensic experience allows us to properly diagnose and mitigate defective construction as it is discovered while navigating competing interests when the restoration work involves multiple stakeholders. This service is among the most challenging in our profession, and we embrace it with enthusiasm.

Construction/Design Defect Litigation

Our experts regularly practice their ability to communicate complicated technical issues with a non-technical audience. We have a broad range of real-world design and construction administration experience. It has proven to be an invaluable asset when investigating deficient construction, and its absence is often a weakness for our competitors.

Emergency Construction Support

The hours after a collapse, and the days after a major catastrophe, are chaotic at best. We understand the need for a practical balance between safety and progress.

Decisive, forward-looking action is critical to (1) stabilize for life safety, (2) mitigate additional property damage, and (3) minimize recovery time. We use our advanced equipment and communication methods to facilitate all three steps of the process, recognizing the value of speed in this environment.

Our Values

Our core values make us unique in our industry, and we consciously return to them for guidance in all decisions – strategic to tactical. We are not a group of individuals that cooperate when convenient; instead, we are a tightly-knit team that comes together daily to share the workload, experience, and support.

Chronic Technophilia

Technology is more than a tool for us; it is a hobby that consumes our nights and weekends. We believe the creative use of new technology will allow us to bring more value in less time on all projects ranging from the ultra-repetitive to the ‘one-offs’. We turn our expensive toys into tools that simultaneously improve the quality and speed of our field work.

For the most part, the forensic engineering service industry is still operating in the Stone Age with PDF reports being prepared manually with a decades-old word processor and data collection methods that date back to the era of film photography. We are redefining the processes and work products, looking toward the digital experiences already developed in parallel fields (e.g. social media, gaming, real estate).

Purposeful Speed

“Does it make sense?” This is the question we ask. Moving with purposeful speed means filling all moments with pragmatic action. We conduct our investigations sensibly and realistically, prioritizing practical over theoretical considerations. Empirical measurements and careful real-world observations are the strongest evidence in an investigation.

Inspiration is drawn from high-tempo military tactics. When we are in the field we are in a race to collect as much quality information as we can, and when we’re in the office we regularly reconsider whether our work product is too much, too little, or just right in relation to our client’s needs, while always keeping an eye on the deadlines and budget.

Physical Fitness

We take great pride in our physical fitness. Only a sound body can have a sound mind, and we know this to be particularly true in post-catastrophe environments with hazardous and uncomfortable conditions. We have the stamina to maintain our focus and execute long after others have surrendered.

No roof is too high, no basement too deep, no wall too thick. The best in our business have the mental and physical strength to ‘go there.’ We are the inquisitive ones that interact with the tradesmen while actually climbing the roof, while the others pontificate abstract theory from the ground below.

Team Commitment

We are not a group of stereotypical ‘lone wolf’ forensic engineers; we are a close-knit pack of coyotes that prefers to take on big challenges together. We want to be part of something greater than ourselves and our own personal interests. The leaders in our team ‘own’ everything in their world—they waste no time looking for others to blame.

We are ‘always on’. We respect each others’ personal time, but when it is time to go, we move. We sacrifice comfort to take a call or travel in pre-dawn and midnight hours because we know our team members are doing the same.

We do not dabble in the core services we offer. This is our full-time pursuit; we don’t have other side gigs distracting us from providing the best quality service.

Humble Interaction

Our services are commonly requested after a building has suffered distress. Mitigation efforts are always costly, and discussions regarding that cost are always delicate, sometimes hostile. We approach the conversation situation with humility; our interaction with all parties is oriented toward the positives of assisting, repairing, and improving.

We are not aloof ivory tower academics disconnected from the practicalities of real-world construction. We deeply respect the knowledge gained from years in the field, and we value opportunities to humbly interact with and learn from experienced tradesmen and maintenance personnel.

Authentic Integrity

Mother nature has a way of constantly surprising us in how she finds ways to literally destroy our buildings, especially when ‘creative’ construction methods are used. Almost anything can and will happen, and this presents countless opportunities to profit from poor communication about the facts, especially after catastrophes. We enforce honesty during our investigations by comprehensively documenting the evidence with precision.

We embrace our ethical obligation to provide our professional services with fairness and objectivity. Our technical opinions lose value if we are perceived to have a bias toward a solution or conclusion that is influenced by factors other than fact-based evidence, the scientific method, and construction industry standards.

Office Locations

Our team holds approximately 110 architect and engineer licenses in 42 states, and we are currently registered to conduct business in 35 states. Our licensed professionals are located throughout the United States, and we are aggressively recruiting to expand professional coverage to all states and U.S. territories.

Although we are based in the United States, our leaders have unique employment backgrounds that allowed for accumulation of decades of design and forensic experience in New Zealand, Australia, Mexico, Puerto Rico, Guatemala, Denmark, Romania, and Dubai. We intensely enjoy the challenge of international work and are always prepared to travel.
